IMDerivatives products automate the options strategies trading cycle, from pre-trade to post-trade, allowing traders to trade as they want, instead of spending time on lower-value order updating and tracking tasks.
Our AI-based market analytics tools bring in unprecedented insights into the evolving market structure, help traders identify previously unseen trading opportunities.
Why
Block options strategies trades nowadays are still arranged “OTC” by brokerage houses. Orders are still matched and executed manually by brokers.
Besides being inefficient, this current human-heavy process can't best serve traders interests in today's markets where greater regulations are common, liquidity is becoming thinner, and market structure keeps involving.

Price discovery takes too long.

Price negotiation takes too long for both traders and brokers. Orders are piling and aging on whiteboards, making it extremely hard to maintain updated quotes from so many parties to so many trades in a continuously moving market.

Will the broker best represent my interests?

Traders cannot be sure that brokers reflected an unbiased picture during negotiations, a broker could easily “protect” or “mislead” one side.

Is my anonymity perserved?

By showing a market upon solicitation, traders release competitive information, and would like to keep anonymous.

Regulatory and compliance requirements.

Global regulations are increasingly requiring a greater transparency for OTC transactions. Therefore traders and brokers have to maintain continuous complex audit trails, which cut across multiple channels (chat, phone, network, etc).

Brokers can't handle heavey volumes.

Brokers can't handle heavy volumes of orders in an efficient way, small and big size orders requiring the same level of attention and service.

Risk management requires realistic price quotes.

End of the month marking is tricky. Traders have to ask the very same brokers they traded with for snapshot quotes of all possible products, and they never get an exhaustive list matching their needs.

Solutions
Equity derivative brokerage industry so far has been minimally disrupted. The need for automated price discovery and trading solution now becomes a necessity.
We provide a suite of solutions that cover the complete spectrum of options strategies, enabling traders to easily manage the end-to-end trading cycle with a fraction of the cost they used to pay.
Full Automation
Fully automate the options strategies trading cycle, covering a wide spectrum of strategies, from REVCON, STRADDLE, to STRANGLE and BUTTERLY, allowing traders to trade whatever they want, whenever they want.
Familiar UI
A familiar instant messaging (aka, chat) UI that allows traders to submit orders in the way they are doing today. A smart market bot that responds to traders' submission and feed traders any market events via the the same UI.
